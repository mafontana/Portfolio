About

Hey, y'all!

I'm originally from Houston and currently call Denver home. I moved to Colorado from Washington DC where I recieved my Master's Degree in Evolutionary Biology at George Washington University and worked as a research associate in the Department of Paleobiology at the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History.


I have worked professionaly as a paleontologist for most of my career, and am now venturing into the exciting world of web development. I am extremely excited about the opportunities a career in tech will provide, and would love to someday apply the skills I learn to the many topics I am passionate about, including science.
